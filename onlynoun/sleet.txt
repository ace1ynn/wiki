Sleet is a regionally variant term for some meteorological phenomena: *Ice pellets, pellets of ice composed of frozen raindrops or refrozen melted snowflakes (United States) *Rain and snow mixed, snow that partially melts as it falls (United Kingdom, Ireland, Canada, and most Commonwealth countries) *Glaze, a smooth coating of ice formed on objects by freezing rain. ==People with the surname== *David Sleet *Don Sleet *Gregory M.
