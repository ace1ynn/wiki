Serine-threonine kinase receptor-associated protein is an enzyme that in humans is encoded by the STRAP gene. == Interactions == STRAP has been shown to interact with: * Mothers against decapentaplegic homolog 2, * Mothers against decapentaplegic homolog 3, * Mothers against decapentaplegic homolog 6, * Mothers against decapentaplegic homolog 7, * TGF beta receptor 1, and * TGF beta receptor 2. == References == == Further reading == * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * .
