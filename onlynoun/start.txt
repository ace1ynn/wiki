STart was a computer magazine covering the Atari ST published from Summer 1986 through April/May 1991—42 issues total. STart began as sections of Atari 8-bit family magazine Antic, before being spun off into a separate publication. Its primarily competitor ST-Log was similarly spawned by ANALOG Computing.
